History

Over a century of serving our community with excellence in healthcare

1995

Foundation

Chaudhary Health Care was founded with a mission to provide quality healthcare to the growing community. Started with just 50 beds and 10 doctors.

2005

First Expansion

The hospital underwent its first major expansion, adding two new wings and increasing capacity to 150 beds. Introduced specialized departments for surgery and internal medicine.

2015

Modernization

Comprehensive modernization project completed, introducing advanced medical technology including CT scanners and establishing the first cardiac care unit in the region.

2020

New Facility

Opened the new state-of-the-art facility with 300+ rooms and 500+ beds. Achieved accreditation as a tertiary care hospital with multiple specialty centers.

2025

Century of Service

Celebrated 100 years of serving the community. Launched telemedicine services and advanced robotic surgery program, continuing our tradition of innovation in healthcare.
